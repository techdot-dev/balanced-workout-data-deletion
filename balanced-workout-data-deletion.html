Balanced Workout - Data Deletion Instructions

This page outlines the steps to permanently delete your Balanced Workout account and all associated data.

Important: Once you delete your account, you will lose access to all your workout data, including past workouts, progress history, and any personalized settings.

Here's how to delete your account:

  1. Open the Balanced Workout app and navigate to the Settings tab located at the bottom of the navigation bar.
  2. Scroll down and locate the "Delete My Account" option.
  3. Tap on "Delete My Account". A confirmation dialog will appear.
  4. Carefully review the information and confirm your decision by tapping on "Delete Account".

Once you confirm deletion, your account will be permanently removed and your data will be erased from our servers.

If you have any further questions regarding data deletion, please feel free to contact our support team at support@balancedworkout.com
